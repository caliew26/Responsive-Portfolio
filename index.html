About Me

Howdy Y'all, my name is Cali and I'm a leo, I like long walks on the beach and drinking Coco-Cola™ (just had to put that, this started off sounding like a dating portfolio, hahahaha). Seriously though, I'm Cali and I was born in Washington State (go Hawks!) many many many moons ago. I have amazing parents and a few okay siblings. I love to smile and laugh, if thats something someone says about me when I'm not there, I'm very happy with that. I want to spread joy and happyness to everyone I meet and everywhere I go.

Family

Have ya ever just went to one of the parks and just sat and watched; so many things to see and enjoy without even moving.
